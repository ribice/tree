---
tagline: Son of Enes, father of Avdikadija
---

Omer was the son of Enes and the father of Avdo Ribić — the kadi (judge) of Jezero known as Avdikadija. His birth year is not recorded; it is estimated at around 1735 (his son Avdikadija was born in 1775), based on an average gap of roughly 40 years between generations in this line.
