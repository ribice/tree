tagline: Son of Enes, father of Avdikadija
---

Omer was the son of [Enes](/en/people/enes-predak) and the father of [Avdo](/en/people/avdikadija) Ribić — the kadi (judge) of Jezero known as Avdikadija. His birth year is not recorded; it is estimated at around 1745 — roughly one generation (~30 years) before his son Avdikadija, who was born in 1775.
