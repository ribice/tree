---
tagline: Teacher and learned man
---

Mehmed, son of Paša, was a teacher — a learned man. He taught in Jezero, and was even a teacher to Rasim Ribić. During the Second World War he lived in Jajce; he is buried in Jezero.

He was called Efendija for his learning, his refined dress and manner. Always elegantly turned out: a white shirt and tie, a new pressed red fez, the most fashionable cane in hand — his bearing thoroughly Viennese, European.
