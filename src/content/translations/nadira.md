Nadira, née Šaranović, is the wife of [Nihad](/en/people/nihad) Ribić. She was born in 1971 in Trepča near Tešanj and is a Bosnian language teacher. With Nihad she has three daughters: [Džana](/en/people/dzana), [Zerina](/en/people/zerina) and [Enida](/en/people/enida).
