---
tagline: "Avdo's wife; from Duljci"
---

Hava Šuškić Ribić, Avdo's wife, was born in Duljci in 1868 and died in 1927. With Avdo she had two children: Esed and Rasim.

She was said to be very tidy, but also proud and quick to take offence. If someone wronged her she could answer sharply — once, when she was »forgotten« off the guest list for a wedding in Lonići, she composed a mocking little song at the guests' expense.
