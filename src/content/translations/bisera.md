tagline: "Akif's daughter; married into Varcar Vakuf"
---

Bisera, Akif's daughter, married a man named Dizdar in Mrkonjić Grad (then Varcar Vakuf). She had a daughter known as »hamma«, who married a Borić and bore Šaćir and Mustafa. Through that line we are related to the Dizdar and Borić families.
