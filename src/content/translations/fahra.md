Fahra Bosnić Ribić, [Rasim's](/en/people/rasim) wife and [Enes's](/en/people/enes) mother, was born in Glamoč in 1903. She was the daughter of Mustafa and [Fatima](/en/people/fatima-ganibegovic) Bosnić of Glamoč; through her mother's Ganibegović line, the family is also related to old families of Jezero and Vražić.

Many family anecdotes survive precisely through her telling. She died on 1 October 1985.
