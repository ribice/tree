Fahra Bosnić Ribić, Rasim's wife and Enes's mother, was born in Glamoč in 1903. She was the daughter of Mustafa and Fatima Bosnić of Glamoč; through her mother's Ganibegović line, the family is also related to old families of Jezero and Vražić.

Many family anecdotes survive precisely through her telling. She died on 1 October 1985.
