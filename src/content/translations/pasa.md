Paša, [Akif's](/en/people/akif) daughter, married Mehaga Hasić in Jezero. She bore three children: [Mujo](/en/people/mujo-hasic), [Mehmed](/en/people/mehmed-hasic) and [Sajd](/en/people/sajd-hasic). Through her we are related to the Hasić family of Jezero.
