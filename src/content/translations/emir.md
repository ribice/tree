Emir, son of [Esad](/en/people/esad) and [Dženana](/en/people/dzenana), born 1991; he has a sister, [Emina](/en/people/emina). From his marriage to Nejra Čerenić (2014–2024) he has a daughter, [Sumejja](/en/people/sumejja) (2015), and a son, [Jakub](/en/people/jakub-emirov) (2018). A great-grandson of Rasim Ribić, he put this family tree online so the whole family could keep and grow it together.
