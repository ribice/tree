Esed, called Eso or Ico, was the younger son of [Izo](/en/people/izo-mladji) the world traveller; his mother was from Jajce. He was a restless child and often in trouble with the law. In one such clash, somewhere in Vojvodina, he killed a policeman, for which he was sentenced to death and executed. By all accounts he was an excellent and talented footballer.
